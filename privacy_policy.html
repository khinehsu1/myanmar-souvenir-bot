Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 90's Doh Ywer ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our automated customer service assistant on Facebook Messenger.

1. Information We Collect

When you send a message to our Facebook Page, we receive:

  • Your Facebook-provided sender ID (a number used to reply to you)
  • The text content of the messages you send us

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, payment details, or any other personal information through this chatbot.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Understand your question about our products
  • Generate and send a helpful reply about prices, stock, and product details

3. Third-Party Services

To provide automated replies, your message text is processed by Google's Gemini AI service. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers or other third parties.

4. Data Retention

We do not permanently store your conversation messages. Messages are processed in real-time to generate a reply and are not saved in a database.

5. Your Rights

You may stop interacting with our chatbot at any time. You can also manage your Messenger data through your Facebook account settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Facebook Page: 90's Doh Ywer.
