# Myanmar Souvenir Bot — Setup Guide
# =====================================
# Follow every step carefully. No coding needed!

## WHAT YOU HAVE
- app.py          → the bot brain (AI + stock data + Messenger logic)
- requirements.txt → list of tools Python needs
- Procfile         → tells Render how to start your bot

## STEP 1 — Upload code to GitHub (free)
1. Go to github.com → Sign up free
2. Click "New repository"
3. Name it: myanmar-souvenir-bot
4. Click "Create repository"
5. Click "uploading an existing file"
6. Upload all 3 files: app.py, requirements.txt, Procfile
7. Click "Commit changes"

## STEP 2 — Deploy on Render (free hosting)
1. Go to render.com → Sign up with GitHub
2. Click "New +" → "Web Service"
3. Connect your GitHub repo: myanmar-souvenir-bot
4. Settings:
   - Name: myanmar-souvenir-bot
   - Runtime: Python 3
   - Build Command: pip install -r requirements.txt
   - Start Command: gunicorn app:app
   - Plan: FREE
5. Click "Advanced" → "Add Environment Variable" — add these one by one:

   Key: GEMINI_API_KEY
   Value: (paste your Gemini API key here)

   Key: PAGE_ACCESS_TOKEN
   Value: (paste your Facebook Page Access Token here)

   Key: APP_SECRET
   Value: (paste your Facebook App Secret here)

   Key: VERIFY_TOKEN
   Value: myanmar_souvenir_verify_2024

6. Click "Create Web Service"
7. Wait 2-3 minutes for it to deploy
8. You will get a URL like: https://myanmar-souvenir-bot.onrender.com
   → SAVE THIS URL!

## STEP 3 — Connect to Facebook Messenger
1. Go to developers.facebook.com → your app
2. Click "Messenger" → "Settings"
3. Scroll to "Webhooks" → Click "Add Callback URL"
4. Callback URL: https://myanmar-souvenir-bot.onrender.com/webhook
5. Verify Token: myanmar_souvenir_verify_2024
6. Click "Verify and Save"
7. Under "Webhooks", click "Add Subscriptions"
8. Check these boxes:
   ✅ messages
   ✅ messaging_postbacks
9. Click "Save"

## STEP 4 — Make your app public
1. In your Facebook app → "App Review" → "Permissions and Features"
2. Request permission for "pages_messaging"
3. Go to "Settings" → "Basic" → change Status to "Live"

## YOU'RE DONE! 🎉
Customers can now message your Facebook Page and the AI will reply
automatically in Burmese or English based on your stock data!

## TROUBLESHOOTING
- Bot not replying? Check your environment variables on Render are correct
- Render goes to sleep after 15 mins of inactivity on free plan
  (first message may take 30 seconds to wake up — this is normal)
- To keep it always awake: use uptimerobot.com (free) to ping your URL every 10 mins
